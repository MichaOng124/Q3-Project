Firelight is a non-profit organiation dedicated to raising awareness about fire safety. Our main goal is to help you create a plan of action to prevent fires from occuring. But more importantly, we train you to survive when one does happen. Fires can occur at any time, and they cause property damage, injury, and even loss of life. Using our online tools, we train you to be perceptive, decisive, and smart about the choices you make during adversities.
